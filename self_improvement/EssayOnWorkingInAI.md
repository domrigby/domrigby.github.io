# Burning Out: An Essay on Working in AI

Date read: 3rd November 2025

[Article link](https://www.interconnects.ai/p/burning-out)

## Key Points
* The window for entering the AI race is closing as frontier performance races forward.
* Researchers are therefore working insanely hard to get their companies in the race (and out of passion).
* It is difficult to know how progress is doing
* Three core ingredients to training cutting edge LLMs:
	1. Internal tools recipes, code-bases etc. Often in house.
	2. Resources: compute and data
	3. Personnel 

* Difficult for companies to catch up and get involved in this race:
	* Researchers often want to innovate when we should be grinding out somethign we know can work.
	* Low hanging fruit is now more expensive and difficult to achieve.
	* Amount of knowledge required to make this work is massive per person.
